The Data Protection Officer ("DPO") is the single point of contact for:

- General privacy enquiries
- Data Subject Requests (DSRs) — access, correction, deletion
- Consent withdrawal
- Data breach reporting during business hours
- PDPC complaints escalation

For after-hours breach reporting, see our [DATA_BREACH_RESPONSE.md](DATA_BREACH_RESPONSE.md).

## How the DPO role works

- Reports to senior management; quarterly briefings to the Board
- Independent authority over privacy decisions
- Has authority to halt processing when necessary
- Maintains the personal-data inventory and DPIA register
